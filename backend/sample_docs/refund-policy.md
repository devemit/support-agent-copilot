# Refund Policy

Customers are eligible for a refund when a duplicate charge is confirmed in the billing system. Support agents must verify the customer account, invoice IDs, payment provider status, and subscription change history before promising a refund.

If duplicate billing is confirmed, agents may offer a refund for the duplicate transaction. If there is only one successful charge and one pending authorization, agents should explain that pending authorizations usually disappear automatically within three to five business days.

Refund requests older than 30 days require escalation to the billing operations team. Agents should not promise refunds for policy exceptions without approval.
