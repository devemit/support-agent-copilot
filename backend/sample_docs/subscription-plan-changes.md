# Subscription Plan Changes

Plan upgrades take effect immediately. When a customer upgrades, the account may receive a prorated charge for the remainder of the billing cycle.

Plan downgrades take effect at the next renewal date unless the customer support team applies an approved exception. Agents should confirm the current plan, previous plan, renewal date, and invoice history before explaining billing changes.

If a customer believes an upgrade caused an incorrect charge, support should compare the upgrade timestamp with the invoice timestamp and check whether a duplicate charge or prorated adjustment occurred.
